ABOUT US

Established in 2015, Marafiki Beach Bar and Restaurant is located by the Jacaranda Bay beach. Great views and fantastic food are offered with a selection of African, Italian and American cuisines. Not to forget the Seafood which plays an important role in our menu! Don't miss out on our masterpiece: the Mixed Grill accompanied with our signature cocktails and drinks. Fresh and local food is what we opt for! We also offer a variety of water-based activities such as Water sports, i.e., kite-surfing, Paddle boarding, boat rides, reef exploring and much more.
